My Philosophy

“Librarian is a service occupation. Gas station attendant of the mind.” -Richard Powers

As an information professional there is no higher honor than the free provision of resources and services that allow people to think, speak, read, write, and act for themselves. To take advantage of all the library has to offer allows people from all walks of life to take advantage of all the world has to offer.
