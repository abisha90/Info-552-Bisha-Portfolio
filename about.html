About Me

  • I grew up in Toronto (Canada) but moved to New York when I was 18, so I *think* my 13 years spent living in the city allow me to claim at least honorary New Yorker status.
  • While in New York I pursued both a bachelor’s and master’s degree in music performance. (I play violin, and am a member of an orchestra in upstate New York.)
  • I’m still pretty new to Philadelphia, but have been enjoying long walks exploring Philly’s iconic landmarks, neighborhoods, and of course, cheesesteak establishments!
